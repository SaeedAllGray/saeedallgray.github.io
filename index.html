Magic FlashCards

A set of educational apps for the young English learners.

We live in a world where learning another language, especially English is vital. Magic Cards 1 is a fun, ad-free and interactive app. Practice reading, listening, and writing to build your vocabulary skills.
Designed by people who have studied the English when they were at the age of ten or so. Therefore, all the features are built based on the materials that a child needs. In other words, this app is designed by the RIGHT people.

Why Magic Cards?


We collected more than 1000 useful words and vocabularies that children should know in the first place. These handy vocabularies would help your child develop a robust learning curve for learning English. Having this complete collection in mind, we designed a unified dictionary which allows you to search for the word, see the definition in picture, listen to the pronunciation and of course, add it to your favorites/bookmarks. This app is one of the eight apps that we are developing. Once your child learned all of the words in this app, he/she can download the next-level vocabularies app (Magic Cards 2) that we would make it available in the near future. (Magic Cards is a set of 8 apps)

Features at glance:

Interactive flash-cards, A fun dictionary, Fun challenges, Inclusive and expressive chart for your progress, NO ADS!, Share your progress with your friends and your teacher, Colorful themes

Features in details:

Our developers have classified the vocabularies by the category. By choosing a category, you would be prompted with the flash-card page. It allows your child to review the worlds and there are three progress bars which show the following information:
how many words your child knows.
how many worlds he/she is reviewing right now.
how many words are in the learning process.

It’s the best practice for your child to examine what he/she has learned. So we designed interactive challenges get a feedback of your child. By taking these challenges, he/she will find out what his/her mistakes are. He/she can get back to flash-cards and practice again. The process of going back and forth between these two stages would reduce the possibility of forgetting the vocabularies. We have also implemented feature in which you can share the your child’s progress with his/her teacher. He/She can share the progress with his/her friends.

Express your character by choosing your favorite color that would be set as the primary theme of the app.

Your status at a glance

A chart is implemented in the app that shows how many words/vocabularies are in process of learning or has been learned before.

Salema Diet Application

A diet-oriented app for the people who need a diet consultant and the folks who want to stay fit.

The very first year of the university was over and by that time, we’d had learned the fundamental concepts of programming and object oriented thinking. To put our knowledge into the practice, we joined a company that was aiming to release a diet-based application at the time. We were a group of 5 computer engineering students which had zero experience in working at the industry. Here are the challenges we confronted and ultimately, solved during a course of three months:
1. By that time, we hadn’t really worked on a real project that’s going to be used by a large number of people.
2. Our employer had asked us to develop and design this application with the Flutter framework. At the time, flutter hasn’t been released for the mass production and it was still in the beta phase just for the developers and the enthusiasts. Being one of the early adapters of a new programming languages means that we have to solve our problem by ourselves or at the worst scenario, ask it from the community. However, of course, there’s a real lack of information in the forums since the community is still under the development. Therefore, we had to have a R&D team dedicated to solving the problems and looking for the best implementations.

Musico

An Open-Source Project

Apple prevents you from using any music extension with built-in Music app. Imagine you have an mp3 file in Whatsapp, You can easily transfer to Musico and continue listening. The aim of this project is to experiment with Swift's APIs and also contribute to the open-source community. Musico supports many music extensions. All you need to have your app supported different file extensions is to add Document Content Type Identifiers in info.plist. More info is available here. Musico is available at Biggboss Repository and you can install it after jailbreaking your iPhone or iPad. You can also review the code at GitHub.
