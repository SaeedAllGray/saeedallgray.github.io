Magic Cards Series

We live in a world where learning another language, especially English is vital. Magic Cards 1 is a fun, ad-free and interactive app. Practice reading, listening, and writing to build your vocabulary skills. Designed by people who have studied the English when they were at the age of ten or so. Therefore, all the features are built based on the materials that a child needs. In other words, this app is designed by the RIGHT people.

Why Magic Cards?

We collected more than 1000 useful words and vocabularies that children should know in the first place. These handy vocabularies would help your child develop a robust learning curve for learning English. Having this complete collection in mind, we designed a unified dictionary which allows you to search for the word, see the definition in picture, listen to the pronunciation and of course, add it to your favorites/bookmarks. This app is one of the eight apps that we are developing. Once your child learned all of the words in this app, he/she can download the next-level vocabularies app (Magic Cards 2) that we would make it available in the near future. (Magic Cards is a set of 8 apps)

Features in details:

Our developers have classified the vocabularies by the category. By choosing a category, you would be prompted with the flash-card page. It allows your child to review the worlds and there are three progress bars which show the following information:
how many words your child knows.
how many worlds he/she is reviewing right now.
how many words are in the learning process.


It’s the best practice for your child to examine what he/she has learned. So we designed interactive challenges get a feedback of your child. By taking these challenges, he/she will find out what his/her mistakes are. He/she can get back to flash-cards and practice again. The process of going back and forth between these two stages would reduce the possibility of forgetting the vocabularies. We have also implemented feature in which you can share the your child’s progress with his/her teacher. He/She can share the progress with his/her friends.

Express your character by choosing your favorite color that would be set as the primary theme of the app.

Your status at a glance

A chart is implemented in the app that shows how many words/vocabularies are in process of learning or has been learned before.
